On dividing 201098 by a certain number, the quotient is 67 and the remainder is 31. Find the divisor.
(A) 3011
(B) 3001
(C) 3021
(D) 2991
 Answer: (B) Explanation: Dividend = (Divisor x Quotient) + Remainder
=> Divisor = (Dividend – Remainder) / Quotient
=> Divisor = (201098 – 31) / 67 = 3001
Thus, B (3001) is the correct choice.Quiz of this Question
