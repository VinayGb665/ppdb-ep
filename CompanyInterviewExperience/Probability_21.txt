From a pack of 52 cards, two cards are drawn together at random. What is the probability of both the cards being kings?
(A) 1/15
(B) 25/57
(C) 35/256
(D) 1/221
 Answer: (D) Explanation: Number of ways to draw 2 cards from a pack of 52 cards = 52C2 = 1326.
Number of ways to draw 2 kings from 4 kings = 4C2 = 6.
Therefore, required probability = 6/1326 = 1/221.
