Round 1: Online Aptitude Test on AMCAT
The first round was an AMCAT test with 4 sections : Quant, Logical, Verbal and Programming MCQs. Many AMCAT sample papers can be found for practice.
Around 50% of the students were eliminated in this round.
Round 2: Technical Round
Questions were asked based on the projects done on resume. Besides that, questions on Java, C, DBMS were asked. Not too difficult. Depends on the panel.
Round 3: HR round
Typical questions such as Strengths, weaknesses, why should we hire you etc.
